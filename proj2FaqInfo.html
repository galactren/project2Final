F.A.Q

Q: How much are the paint jobs?
A:For candy coats the estimate is $1,200 for a sedan and coupe. Suvs and larger vehicles will be an additional $1,000. Smaller vehicles such as motorcycles for complete fairing paint jobs in candy coats will be $1,000. Matte finishes will start at $1,300 with an addition of $800 for larger vehicles. For motorcycles, it will be $1,000. Chameleon prices start at $2,500 with an additional $1,000 for larger vehicles. For motorcycles, it will be $1,200. Of course, these are all estimates for work. So please come on down or contact us for a precise estimate.

Q: How long will it take for the job to be completed?
A:It usually takes about 2-3 weeks for small vehicles. 3-6 weeks for large vehicles. Motorcycles about 3-4 weeks.

Q: Is there a deposit I need to put down?
A:Yes, We require a 15% NON-REFUNDABLE deposit of an estimated price to begin work.

Q: Do you offer to finance?
A:Yes! we offer financing through major banks and third-party lenders.

Q:Can I get something else painted? Such as a Boat, RV, Trailer, or Plane?
A:Yes! We offer services for those vehicles and equipment as well. Contact us for estimates and finish suggestions!

Q: Do y'all do alternative work besides paint jobs?
A:We offer alternative methods to get your ride looking great! such as plasti-dip, hyro-dip, 3M wraps, and single panel work.
